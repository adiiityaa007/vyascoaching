Terms and Conditions

Vyas Coaching Classes - Terms and Conditions

1. Admission Policy

Admission to Vyas Coaching Classes is granted based on the availability of seats. All admissions are processed on a first-come, first-served basis. To confirm your admission, the student must complete the registration process and fulfill the initial fee payment requirement. Vyas Coaching Classes reserves the right to deny admission to students if the batch is full or if the admission criteria are not met.

2. Fee Structure and Payment Policy

The fee for the selected course must be paid either in full at the time of admission or in two installments, as per the structured plan below:

  • Full Payment: Students may choose to pay the entire course fee at the time of registration.
  • Installment Payment Option:
    • 1st Installment: 50% of the total course fee must be paid at the time of admission to confirm enrollment.
    • 2nd Installment: The remaining 50% must be paid within 30 calendar days from the date of admission.

Please ensure that all payment receipts are retained safely for future reference. Payments can be made via cash, UPI, or bank transfer. For bank transfers, students are required to share the transaction details with the administration office for confirmation.

3. Refund Policy

Once the classes have commenced, no refund of fees will be entertained under any circumstances. This includes situations such as withdrawal by the student, inability to attend classes, or any personal issues. Students are advised to consider their schedule and availability before confirming admission.

4. General Conduct

All students are expected to maintain discipline and attend classes regularly. Any kind of misconduct or disruption in the class environment may lead to cancellation of admission without any refund.
